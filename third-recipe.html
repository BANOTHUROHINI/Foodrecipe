Sandwich

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and Sandwich. This top-rated sandwich is easy to make and it comes together in just 5 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast. Learn how to make, store, and serve the best sandwich ever. .

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Sandwich breakfast meat food

instructions

step 1: Mix

Combine your dry ingredients (bread, soya sauce, cream, tomato) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (spinach, bread, chesse, cucumber) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.

step 2: cook

Toast the bread (if desired) and spread the chosen spread on one or both slices of bread. Layer the deli meat, cheese, or vegetarian protein on top of one of the slices of bread. Add the lettuce or other greens on top of the protein layer. Add the tomato or other vegetables on top of the greens. Season with salt and pepper (if desired). Top the sandwich with the other slice of bread, with the spread side facing down. Cut the sandwich in half, if desired, and serve.

step 3: serve

Serve your Sandwich immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite sandwich toppings.

ingredients

2 slices of bread

1-2 tbsp of spread (such as mayonnaise, mustard, hummus, pesto, or cream cheese)

2-3 slices of deli meat, cheese, or vegetarian protein (such as tofu or tempeh)

1-2 leaves of lettuce or other greens

1-2 slices of tomato or other vegetables (such as cucumber or avocado)

Salt and pepper to taste (optional)

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid
