Vegetable Soup

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and vegetable soup. This top-rated vegetable soup is easy to make and it comes together in just 15 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast. Learn how to make, store, and serve the best Vegetable soup ever. .

prep time

35 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

8 servings

Tags : vegetables breakfast soup food

instructions

step 1: Mix

Combine your dry ingredients (carrot, cucumber, mirchi, tomato) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (spinach, tomato, chesse, cucumber) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.

step 2: cook

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened. Add the chopped carrots, celery, and potatoes, and sauté for another 5 minutes until they start to soften. Add the chopped vegetables, canned tomatoes, and dried herbs, and stir to combine. Pour in the vegetable broth or water, and bring the soup to a simmer. Let the soup simmer for 20-30 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have developed. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh herbs or croutons if desired.

step 3: serve

Serve your vegetable Soup immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite soup toppings.

ingredients

1 onion, diced

2-3 cloves of garlic, minced

2-3 carrots, chopped

2-3 celery stalks, chopped

1-2 potatoes, peeled and diced

1-2 cups of chopped vegetables (such as broccoli, cauliflower, green beans, or zucchini)

1 can of diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)

6-8 cups of vegetable broth or wate

1 tsp dried herbs (such as thyme, oregano, or basil)

Salt and pepper to taste

1-2 tbsp of olive oil or butter (optional)

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons
